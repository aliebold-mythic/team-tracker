This app connects to the Google Sheet named 'Mission Month 2026 Tracker'. If the app crashes, check that the Service Account email (in Streamlit Secrets) is still an 'Editor' on that Sheet.

---

How it works:
Click the link (works on mobile or desktop).

Enter your Name and select the type of contribution (Volunteer Hours or Money).

Click Submit.

Privacy Note
Inputs are Private: Individual entries are hidden. No one else on the team can see that you specifically donated $50 or worked 2 hours.

Our Impact is Public: The dashboard only displays the Grand Totals (e.g., "Team Total: 150 Hours") so we can celebrate our progress together.
